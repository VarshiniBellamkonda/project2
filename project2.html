The famous Palate Fest is back with its latest edition, in collaboration with Mirchi Live, bigger and better than ever before!
The two day food extravaganza is coming to satiate discerning palates across all ages with a host of gastronomical delights, and is taking place at a brand new venue, the versatile Aerocity Grounds at GMR Aerocity.
The fest will be on from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM on the February 4 and February 5, 2023.

Palate Fest

Free Entry|Pets are not allowed

The food festival will have stalls set up by leading names in the culinary space, including from the kitchens of five star hotels in the city, like the Hyatt, Soulinaire by IHCL, Ambassador and more.
Also find Koyla Kebab, Spice Market, The Great Kebab Factory, Burgerama, Something's Brewing, Wok In The Cloud, Kylin, Art Of Dumpling, Masala Story, Blue Tokai and more.
